Fruit Milk

  • Prep Time : 5 mins
  • Total Time : 5 mins
  • Serving : 2

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup strawberries
  2. 1 banana
  3. ½ cup yogurt
  4. ¼ cup pineapple juice
  5. 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar

Directions

Step 1 Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender; blend until smooth.
